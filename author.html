Hey everyone, it's your boy strikermi9, also known as Benjamin Nunoo.
Welcome to my website, AstoFantasySec.com, or "AFS" for short.

It's where I share the games I've created, showcase my projects (anything related to games),
and keep you updated on what I'm doing.

Creating this site was a mini project that I thought would be fun and interesting.

Let me tell you a bit about myself.
I’m a 22-year-old university student at RAVEUNILDN, studying game programming to improve my skills
in Unreal Engine and Unity, as well as coding.
My goal is to become a game developer, focusing on game design and programming.
I would describe myself as hardworking, punctual, prepared, and eager to learn new things
that will benefit both me and others.

The work experience I've gained has taught me various coding languages, including HTML and CSS,
and provided me with the experience of making a game as part of a team.
I previously attended DigiLab (now known as Shapeways.io) for work experience,
and after that, I returned to improve my HTML and CSS skills.
I had the opportunity to work with others and even assisted as a coding teacher in a few sessions
to help teach youth club kids HTML and CSS.
I used to play youth soccer on two teams, AFC Wandsworth and Sporting Duet Academy,
