This beta built game was created for my first project
for my Year 2 UAL extended Diploma. This was a concept i thought
up from one of the things I brought up on a story/desprition about myself
which I had take some ideas from it to make game out of that idea.I had some
others ideas but this was the one I decided to focus on and create.

This game is based on me helping grandma(Nonna) do grocery
going to places like tooting or brixtion where I follow her around carry
what she buys in her trolly stroller.The gameplay for the Idea it is a catching
game and what you have to do is to catch as many food Nonna throws at you while moving
side to side, while the time ran out. You get points for every food you catch and you're
trying to get a highscore.
All from the sprite to scenery I designed my self.
