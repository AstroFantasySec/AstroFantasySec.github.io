Description

This game was created for one of my UAL course units.
It's an open world game called The Vie Eleccion.
The world is similar to ours, where your choices (Eleccion) determine whether you become a law-abiding citizen or a criminal.
This is a beta version (beta_Vers 1.0) with hints of alpha — not a completed game.
It includes scenery, character movement, a weapon system, and some functional mechanics.
You can watch the video above or try it yourself using the download link below.
