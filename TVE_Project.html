This game was built for my Year 1 UAL game design Diploma Final Major Project.
This was a concept i thought up on the spot last 3 months on Ual year 1 course at another school
, on a march when I finished whatever units I was doing berfore. That project wasn't easy
It was base took three weeks , and they didnt gave me time to prepare and rush me when I had other
work to do. But it was project to work on and it's and I was commited to put time on it and get as much
on it.

I originally had two ideas and I was going to make it a two in one game, one big important main face porject
and the other smnall mini game you can access it on the menu. But when started the Game 1 I realized I wouldnt have
time to create both making choose Game 1 plus I already started the production of the game. Game 1 the game I was
I was working on is a Open world game influence to similiar games like gta, watch dogs, saint row , apb etc, but with spin.
What make this game stand out you can decide to hero or crimial kinda like APB where you be a enforcer or criminal but in this game
instead of being just enforcer or criminal , you be your own person and choose to do good things to benfit your city even if you have to get your
hand dirty or do crimminal stuff which will be frown upon at the same time. You can do whatever you want and choose your own path like the game
Shadow the Hedghog (Which I also got some influence from), where you can Choose your path in game Good, neutral and evil,
Whatever descion you choose will determined your ending.

I wanted the game enviroment to be influence by america cities/states mostlylike Southern like Baton rouge,LA/Texas and
some East coast cities/state like new york/chicago. The name of the city I created is called Charlston(M)RougeVille,
An American sounded name. So far I was able to decent size area of the city, it kinda look like a town.
The name I chose for this game is The vie eleccion, the reason is that name is made out of three languages, English , French and Spanish
Showin the communties influence like this is a fictional american city (english) , (french) small influnces because of the city history and
(spanish) its America of course their going be some Latin communties along side black/white communties as well. The name translate , the life choice
